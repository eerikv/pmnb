QUALITY MANAGEMENT

Quality Management is the process that aims to develop and manage the quality of software so that we can ensure that the product meets.

  • the quality standards expected by our customers.
  • the necessary regulatory and developer requirements.

It is a disciplined approach to overseeing and enhancing all aspects of software development, deployment, and maintenance to ensure the final product meets or exceeds user expectations. It encompasses processes, standards, and practices that focus on functionality, performance, reliability, and user satisfaction, ultimately creating high-quality software products. It aims to create a working environment where quality is the responsibility of everyone on the team.
